Two elevation tiers. Quiet cream/content cards rest flat (fill + hairline) and lift −4px on hover with the soft --shadow-hover (0 12px 32px /.07). Full-bleed image cards (work covers, the All Projects grid) take the deeper --shadow-image (0 16px 40px /.18) — a heavier lift that reads against photography — plus a bottom-up dark protection gradient for white-text legibility.
